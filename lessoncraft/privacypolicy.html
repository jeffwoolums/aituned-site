Privacy Policy

LessonCraft by Gospel Tuned · Last updated February 13, 2026

What We Collect

LessonCraft collects minimal data to provide you with a great experience:

  • Account Information — Your name, email address, and authentication credentials when you create an account.
  • Lesson Data — Lessons, talks, and study notes you create within the app. This data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced via iCloud.
  • Usage Analytics — Anonymous usage patterns (features used, session duration) to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is included.
  • Device Information — Device type, OS version, and app version for compatibility and support purposes.

How We Use Your Data

  • To generate personalized lessons, study guides, and audio content using Gospel Tuned AI.
  • To save and sync your created content across your devices.
  • To send notifications you've opted into (Morning Light devotionals, Come Follow Me reminders).
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs.
  • We never sell your personal data to third parties.

AI Content Generation

When you generate lessons or study content, your topic and preferences are sent to our AI service to create personalized material. We do not store your generated content on our servers after delivery — it lives on your device.

Audio narration is generated using text-to-speech technology. Generated audio files are cached locally for offline listening.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple — App Store, iCloud sync, push notifications.
  • Stripe — Payment processing for subscriptions and print orders. We never see or store your full credit card number.
  • Printful — Print fulfillment for art orders. Your shipping address is shared with Printful solely to deliver your order.
  • Gospel Tuned AI — Our AI backend for content generation.

Data Storage & Security

  • Your lessons and preferences are stored locally using Apple's secure on-device storage.
  • iCloud sync (when enabled) uses Apple's end-to-end encrypted CloudKit infrastructure.
  • We use industry-standard encryption for all data transmitted between the app and our servers.
  • We do not have access to your iCloud-synced data.

Your Rights

  • Export — You can export all your data at any time from Settings → Export All Data.
  • Delete — You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting support@tredtechnologies.com.
  • Opt Out — You can disable notifications, analytics, and iCloud sync independently in Settings.

Children's Privacy

LessonCraft is designed for users of all ages in a family-friendly environment. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.
